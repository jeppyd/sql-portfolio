Questions answered:

1.) Which campaigns were the most successful? Which were the least?
-The email promo blast, re-engagement, and welcome series boasted incredible ROI, likely due to low cost and high exposure to existing customers with shorter funnel.
2747%, 2655%, and 2605% were the respective ROIs. 

-The least successful were the three meta campaigns- lookalike, retargeting, and awareness Q1.
-All 3 suffered negative ROIs: -59%, -62%, and -70% respectively.





2.)What other trends were noticed?
-We went over budget on literally everything- so that's not good. Finance will take care of that though.... right?
Seriously though, next time it will be wise to reallocate funds from clearly ineffective campaign strategies into more profitable ones. 
Lastly, there were general spikes in revenue in the months of April and August due to overlapping campaigns- care should be taken differentiate these in the future.
